Prime Live Event Katie Melua

Cadogan Hall, London
25 Jul 2017 and 26 Jul 2017

Event description

Returning to London for her first UK concerts of 2017, Katie Melua and her band will perform for only 770 fans at Chelsea’s stunning artistic landmark, Cadogan Hall. Katie is one of Britain’s most successful recording artists of the millennium, selling more than 11 million albums, one million concert tickets and receiving 56 platinum awards.

This series of two very intimate and unique appearances will see Katie walk the audience through songs from her extensive catalogue--including new album In Winter--by way of personal anecdotes, insights into her creative process, and stories from her illustrious career as a recording artist.

Check out Katie Melua’s artist store for albums, playlists featuring a host of her biggest hits and more.

Cadogan Hall

5 Sloane Terrace, Belgravia, London, SW1X 9DQ
Audience size: 770

Starting life as a New Christian Science Church in 1907, Cadogan Hall is now home to the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra, and the world-famous BBC Proms Chamber Music series. Prized for its excellent acoustics and luxurious surroundings, it has become one of London’s leading venues. Since re-opening as a concert hall in 2004, Cadogan Hall continues to offer a vibrant selection of contemporary, jazz and folk music events as well as talks and conferences. In July, singer-songwriter Katie Melua will bring her tales of love and life to the venue as part of Amazon’s Prime Live Events series.
